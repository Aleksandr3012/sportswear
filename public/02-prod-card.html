ABOUT BRAND

The new GEL-Nimbus 22 model, which is part of the top ASICS line, is endowed with greater comfort, stability and speed than its previous versions.

The jacquard mesh was chosen as the material of the top, with a thoughtful pattern in strategic zones, which allows you to adjust the degree of breathability and fit of the foot.

ASICS engineers equipped these sneakers with a modified Gel shock-absorbing block in the heel and added 2 millimeters of energy-resistant Flytefoam Propel to the midsole.

Stability of movement is supported by Trusstic technology, which reinforces the outer region of the midfoot and counteracts the twisting effect.

The highly abrasive Ahar + rubber is located in the zones of maximum outsole load, which are located both in the heel part and in the toe area.

GEL-Nimbus 22 is a model of reliability and comfort for runners with a neutral foot position, who prefer running from the heel and midfoot.

In these sneakers, you can run a distance of any length.

  • adaptive fit;
  • jacquard mesh;
  • Socklining Eva OrthoLite — lightweight, soft, durable, comfortable molded insole made of Eva polymer;
  • Gel — technology of depreciation and shock absorption in the heel and toe;
  • Trusstic System — a thermoplastic insert under the elevator of the foot provides stability, reduces weight and extends the life of the sole;
  • Flytefoam Propel — the upper layer of the sole creates a high degree of recoil during deformation, providing a springy effect;
  • Flyte Foam — improved sole technology, the lightest material in the history of the brand, excellent cushioning and energy return;
  • 3d Full Contact — the design covers the entire arch of the foot, maximum contact with the surface in each phase of running;
  • Ahar + - high-carbon rubber, developed by ASICS, anti-abrasion of soles in places of maximum load.
